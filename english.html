About me

Hello, my name is Patrick Pineault, I am 22 years old. I am passionate about computers and technology. I started developing an interest in this field when I helped my classmates and teachers with various computer-related issues during my high school studies. Later on, I decided to do the Vocational Diploma in computer support to deepen my knowledge and find the path that suits me best in this field.

During my studies, the web development course particularly appealed to me. That's why, after my Vocational Diploma, I chose to enroll in the AEC (Attestation of College Studies) in web development to learn more.

Today, I am looking for a job in this field. I will therefore present you with an overview of some of my web development projects.
